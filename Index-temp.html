The album The Woods by A Swarm of the Sun is glacially beautiful. Despite moving at the pace of summer glacial melt, the latest release by A Swarm of the Sun is incredibly captivating. At times a minimalist dream, this is post-rock romanticism at it's finest.

My Ravings

I have listened to this album well over 200 times since it's release. This is my go-to album when I don't know what to listen to, I need to relax, I practice yoga, or I'm training aerial or handbalancing.

I LOVE THIS ALBUM!!!
